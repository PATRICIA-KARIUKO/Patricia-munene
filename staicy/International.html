GETTING HERE FROM ABROAD

Winter College mandates that international students acquire an International Student Pass and Visa to enroll in educational programs. Applicants must secure acceptance as students at an accredited Educational or Training Establishment before initiating the application process. Visit this link to review the student pass application requirements. For further assistance, reach out to @wintercollege.edu, a college specializing in technology, IT, software development, and innovation.
